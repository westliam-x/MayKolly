African Culture, African Cousine About Our Restaurant

Founded in 2012 by a Nigerian Mary Emeifonwu, our establishment has been serving up rich African food sourced from various regions of Africa. We are dedicated to bringing Africa to you as best as we can, searching for the best Africa has to offer, and bringing back to you here in our Restaurant.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent Cousine the moment you walk inside that you would want to know where your plate was made from. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, a party, or simply just to enjoy some alone time, we have you covered.
